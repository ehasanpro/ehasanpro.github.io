Common Questions

Everything you need to know about working with me

How do you charge for your services?
Pricing varies by service: articles start at $400, email sequences at $800, landing pages at $1,500, and complete websites at $4,000. Monthly retainers offer discounted rates for ongoing work.
What's your typical turnaround time?
Single articles take 7-10 days, email sequences 10-14 days, landing pages 2-3 weeks, and complete websites 4-6 weeks. Rush delivery available for urgent projects.
Do you offer monthly retainers?
Yes! Monthly retainers are ideal for ongoing content needs like blog posts, emails, or LinkedIn ghostwriting. Retainers come with discounted rates and priority scheduling.
How many revisions are included?
Most services include 2-3 rounds of revisions. My goal is to deliver exceptional work from the start, but I'm committed to refining until you're 100% satisfied.
What if I'm not sure which service I need?
Book a free 15-minute consultation. We'll discuss your goals, and I'll recommend the best approach for your specific situation.
