Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about website copywriting

What's included in the messaging framework?

Your messaging framework includes your positioning statement, unique value proposition, key messages, brand voice guidelines, and messaging hierarchy. It's the strategic foundation for all your communications.

Do you work with existing brand guidelines?

Yes! If you have existing brand guidelines, I'll work within them. If not, I'll create voice and tone guidelines as part of the process to ensure messaging consistency.

Can you write for technical/specialized industries?

Absolutely. I've worked with SaaS, healthcare, fintech, legal, and other specialized industries. I invest time in understanding your industry so the copy is accurate and credible.

What if I need updates after launch?

I offer post-project support packages for updates and revisions. Many clients also retain me on a monthly basis for ongoing website and marketing copy needs.

Do you provide SEO optimization?

Yes, I incorporate SEO best practices including keyword research and optimization while keeping copy natural and conversion-focused. The priority is human readers first, search engines second.
