Frequently Asked Questions

What email service providers (ESPs) do you work with?

I work with all major ESPs including Mailchimp, ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, HubSpot, Klaviyo, MailerLite, and more. I provide copy that's ready to paste into any platform, plus setup guidance specific to your ESP.

Do you write subject lines?

Absolutely! Each email includes 3 subject line options tested against proven formulas. Subject lines are crucial for open rates, so I put significant effort into creating compelling, click-worthy options that avoid spam filters.

Can you help with email design/templates?

I focus on copywriting, but I provide clear formatting guidance and can recommend where images, buttons, and CTAs should go. I can also connect you with email designers if you need template design services.

How do you measure email success?

I track open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and revenue per email. I provide recommendations for what metrics to monitor in your ESP and how to continuously improve performance through data-driven optimization.

Do you offer monthly retainers?

Yes! Monthly retainers are perfect for ongoing email needs like newsletters, promotional campaigns, and seasonal sequences. You get discounted per-email rates, priority scheduling, and consistent communication. Contact me for custom retainer packages.
