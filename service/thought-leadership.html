Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure the content reflects my expertise and voice?

I start every project with a detailed discovery call to understand your perspective, experiences, and communication style. I also review your existing content and incorporate your feedback through revision rounds to ensure the final piece authentically represents your voice.

What topics work best for thought leadership?

The best topics combine your unique expertise with your audience's pressing questions. I help identify topics where you have contrarian insights, practical frameworks, or data-driven perspectives that stand out from generic industry content.

How long does it take to see results?

Thought leadership is a long-term strategy. You may see immediate engagement on individual pieces, but building authority typically takes 3-6 months of consistent publishing. The compound effect means each piece continues generating value over time.

Can you help with content distribution?

Yes! I provide social media snippets, email newsletter versions, and publishing guidance. For ongoing clients, I can develop complete distribution strategies to maximize reach and engagement.

Do you offer monthly retainers?

Absolutely. Monthly retainers include discounted per-article rates, priority scheduling, and strategic content planning. Contact me to discuss a custom package based on your publishing frequency.
