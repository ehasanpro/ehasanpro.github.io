Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which service I need?

Not sure? Book a free 15-minute consultation call and we'll discuss your goals, challenges, and which service (or combination of services) would be most effective for your business.

What's your typical turnaround time?

It varies by service: single emails take 3-5 days, articles 7-10 days, landing pages 10-14 days, and complete websites 2-4 weeks. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer package deals or retainers?

Yes! Monthly retainers offer discounted rates and priority scheduling. Common packages include ongoing blog content, regular email campaigns, or consistent LinkedIn ghostwriting. Contact me for custom pricing.

How many revisions are included?

Most services include 2-3 rounds of revisions. My goal is to get it right the first time, but I'm committed to refining until you're completely satisfied with the final product.

Can you write for technical or specialized industries?

Absolutely. I have experience writing for SaaS, fintech, healthcare, and other technical fields. I invest time in research to ensure accuracy and credibility in your specific industry.
